Ascending Melodies - Henry Balducci

As you could assume by the name, in this melody the pitches are ascending. This kind of melody brings a happy feel to song and is very up beat.

    Examples of ascending melodies

  • "Stairway to Heaven" -- Led Zeplin
  • "Can't Help Falling in Love" -- Elvis Presley
  • "Sir Duke" -- Stevie Wonder
