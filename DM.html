Descending Melodies - Henry Balducci

Similar to a ascending melody, the pitches are descending. This can add a gloomy feel to a song.

Examples of descending melodies

  • "Fly Me to the Moon" -- Frank Sinatra
  • "Moon River" -- Andy Williams
  • "My Heart Will Go On" -- Celine Dion
