Melody - Henry Balducci

The melody is naturally the heart of the song. Having a distinct pattern of notes, the melody is what makes a listener remember a song, even if they trip up on the words.

  • There are two main components that make up a good melody. The pitch and the rhythm.

  • The pitch of the melody refers to the particular notes expressed within a melody. Melodies can rest on the same note or pitch, it can also change pitch from note to note but is often a mixture of both.

  • The rhythm is how the specific notes of a melody are expressed on the beat. The melodies of a bass line are generally rhythmic.
