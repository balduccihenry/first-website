Jump and Skip Melodies - Henry Balducci

Jump and skip melodies are probably the most common melody you will encounter when making music. Instead of moving in a preictable up and down apttern like an ascending or descending melody, these melodies will move from one direction to another in the same song.

Examples of jump and skip melodies

  • "Yesterday" -- The Beatles
  • "Bohemian Rhapsody" -- Queen
  • "I Want to Hold Your Hand" -- The Beatles
